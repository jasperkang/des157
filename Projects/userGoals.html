User Goals

How will your users understand the content of the project?
The title of the website: "How Things Are Made" gives an initial understanding of the content of the project. In addition, I will give a short summary in the home page of how the website works, and what it's function is.

How will your users understand the primary objectives?
The primary objectives will be explained in the home page summary.

What content and features do your users need, and in what form do they need it?
This website shows how certain materials are made and the lifecycle analysis of each material. There should be a navigation to search each material.

How do your users think your interactive projects should work and what are their experience levels with the type of media you are creating? This interactive project should be intuitive and easy to operate. It is designed for all age groups so that users of all ages can easilt understand the content. I seek to create each material to be an interactive learning experience that should be fun to use.
